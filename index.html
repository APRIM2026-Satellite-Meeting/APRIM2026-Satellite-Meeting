APRIM2026 Satellite Meeting

Precision Engineering for Space Exploration: Robotics, Autonomy and Instrumentation for Lunar, Planetary and Small Body Missions

Date: May 3, 2026 (Sunday)
Venue: M1603 and M1607, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University
Format: In-person with online streaming option

Meeting Overview

This one-day satellite meeting is organized in conjunction with APRIM2026 in Hong Kong. It focuses on the intersection of precision engineering, robotics, autonomous systems, and instrumentation for lunar, planetary, and small body exploration missions.

The meeting aims to foster exchange among researchers in astronomy, planetary science, robotics, autonomy, and space engineering, while highlighting Hong Kong’s research strengths in deep space exploration technologies.
